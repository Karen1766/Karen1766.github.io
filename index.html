Welcome to Kare More Yoga

I offer virtual consultations through Zoom to help design personalized programs for you to reach your fitness goals and research-based yoga and training sessions.

I aim to work with individuals with chronic pain or non-conventional physical needs. Many fitness programs are designed for a general population. But if you are looking for something specified for your health and your needs, and want to move beyond pre-recorded videos Kare More Yoga may be for you. When possible, I address the possible root issues of chronic pain. I always work to strengthen you around your needs. My programs are rooted in the posture-supporting principles of yoga and research-based scientific kinesthetic principles. Program structure will also be influenced by the guidelines set by the ACSM (American College of Sports Medicine) for developing muscular and cardiovascular strength and endurance.

About

  • B.S. Kinesiology: Exercise Science
  • 200 Hr Certified Yoga Instructor
  • CPR Certified

I studied Kinesiology, with a focus in Exercise Science at California State University, Long Beach. There, I worked in the Physiology of Exercise (PEXS) Lab and assisted with a variety of kinesiology-based research studies. I also worked with a health education outreach program, Wellness Warriors, during this time.

I earned my Yoga Instructor Certification from Cindy Zackney, formerly of Kava Yoga in Long Beach, CA. Our certification focused on correct posture and joint alignment in the styles of Hatha and Vinyasa (or Flow) yoga, across levels. Safety and instructional cues were paramount.

I also recently began designing supplemental training programs for the CSULB Archery Team, which is one of the oldest collegiate archery programs in the country. I am currently working on becoming certified as an ACSM exercise physiologist, and am planning on attending physical therapy school.
